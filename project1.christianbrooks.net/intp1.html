Short Bio

I was born in Bay County Florida to my parents Shawn and Michelle Brooks. I grew up in East Georgia and was homeschooled until 9th grade. I attended Kings Way Christian School from 10th grade to 12th grade. I graduated as Valedictorian. Soon after graduation, I traveled to Fort Leonard Wood Missouri for Basic Training and Military Police School. After 22 weeks, I was officially a Military Police Officer.

After making it through one of the most difficult 5 months of my life, I decided to go to college to become an officer in the US Army. A journey which would make those 5 months look like a cake walk.
